Debt Collection
Triple OK Law provides ancillary services in debt collection and debt recovery.
Our polite, persuasive, persistent but professional approach produces better debt recovery results than traditional strong armed-tactics. We subscribe to the ethical standards in debt collection and comprehend that the debt collection is part of a continuing relationship between client and debtor. Special attention is laid to case study, evaluation and assessment, establishing communication. We are not tied down to a particular method, whilst rendering with professionalism and care.
